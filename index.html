Welcome to Pelican Picks – EU wide ecommerce trading

At Pelican Picks, we are proud to be your reliable partner in online commerce, connecting customers across Europe with premium products in perfume & beauty, electronics, toys and more. As a dynamic online retailer, we operate on leading marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, and others, offering a seamless and trustworthy shopping experience.
